Munibot: Friendly Twitter Geo-Bots

Munibot is a library to build Twitter bots that post random aerial or satellite imagery of administrative regions (tipically municipalities).
Current active bots are:
  • Municipios de España (@munibot_es)
  • Communes de France (@communebot)
  • Municipis de Catalunya (@munibot_cat)
Munibot is a project by Adrià Mercader.
